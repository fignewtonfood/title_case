3) Multiple words to TC w/ exceptions     george goes to the store      George Goes to the Store
4) Intraword exceptions to TC             george maccloud               George MacCloud
5) All Caps to TC                         GEORGE                        George
6) Partial Caps to TC                     gEoRgE                        George
7) Special characters to TC               17 georges!                   17 Georges!
8) Other exceptions to TC                 ke$ha                         Ke$ha
